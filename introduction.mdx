## Overview

JSON Viewer AI empowers you to handle JSON data effortlessly. Paste any JSON into the tool at [https://jsonviewerai.com](https://jsonviewerai.com), and it instantly formats, validates, and visualizes your data. You gain tree views, syntax highlighting, and error detection without installing software. Developers, testers, and data analysts use it daily to debug APIs, review payloads, and share readable JSON.

This free online tool processes complex structures in seconds, supporting files up to 10MB. No accounts or sign-ups required—start immediately.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Paste Your JSON" icon="clipboard">
    Open [https://jsonviewerai.com](https://jsonviewerai.com) and paste your JSON data into the editor.

````json
{"user":{"name":"Alice","age":30,"hobbies":["reading","coding"]},"settings":{"theme":"dark"}}
````
  </Step>
  <Step title="View and Validate" icon="eye">
    The tool auto-parses and highlights. Errors appear in red with fix suggestions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Beautify and Export" icon="download">
    Click `Beautify` for formatted output. Copy, download as `.json`, or share via URL.
  </Step>
</Steps>
